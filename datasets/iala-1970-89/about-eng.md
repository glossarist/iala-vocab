# About the IALA Dictionary 1970–1989 Edition

The original IALA Dictionary was compiled over two decades, from 1970 to 1989, representing the foundational work of international maritime experts. This comprehensive edition established the baseline terminology for marine aids to navigation, covering essential concepts and definitions that have remained relevant for decades.

The 1970–1989 edition serves as a historical reference and demonstrates the evolution of maritime terminology during a critical period of standardization.

For the authoritative source, visit the [IALA Dictionary](https://www.iala.int/wiki/dictionary/) on the official IALA website.
