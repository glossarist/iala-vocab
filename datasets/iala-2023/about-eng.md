# About the IALA Dictionary 2023 Revision

The 2023 Revision of the IALA Dictionary represents a modern update to the original work, incorporating contemporary developments in maritime technology, navigation systems, and international standards. This revised edition reflects current practices in electronic navigation, vessel traffic services, automatic identification systems, and emerging technologies in maritime safety.

The 2023 Revision ensures that the IALA Dictionary remains a current and authoritative resource for maritime professionals.

For the authoritative source, visit the [IALA Dictionary](https://www.iala.int/wiki/dictionary/) on the official IALA website.
